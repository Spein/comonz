THE COMONZ SPIRIT

The web is full of free contents that counts ! Wouldn't it be fair to offer support to all of these content creators who make us feel the internets every day? We provide a donation-based system that let you retribute them in a fair and personalized way.

No Charges or Ads

Tired of giving half your money to big platforms, or selling your souls while watching retargeted ads? We take a minimistic 10%-fee.. just for our dev's to eat and pay their bills. Everything else go directly in your favorite creators' pockets.

Freedom

Time is money, we let you choose both of them. How much do you want to donate this month? After how many seconds do you consider a creation deserve your money? We let you determine the basic conditions of your support and start retributing their hard work without noticing it.

Painless

Just watch, read, listen, thrill, laugh and share the internets as you would always do. The extension just start a timer with the duration you've picked earlier. If you're enjoying until the end of it, we automaticaly secure a fraction of your wallet for that creator..Oh and you can attach a comment to each donation you make!

Fair to you and creators

We'll retribute each creator you've supported with an equal part of your whole wallet. Let's say you've been supporting contents from 5 differents creators last month, they will receive one fifth of your wallet each. We only keep 5% in commissions to ensure the continuity of our services
